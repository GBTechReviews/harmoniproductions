HCloud — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

HCloud is a HarmonyOS app that lets you back up your own photos, videos, and files to your own Google Drive and/or Google Photos account. HCloud has no servers of its own — it connects your device directly to Google's services using your Google sign-in.

What we access

With your permission, Harmoni Cloud can:

  • Upload photos, videos, and files you choose to your Google Drive, in a folder named "HCloud" or "HCloud Files".
  • Upload photos and videos you choose to your Google Photos library.

What we don't do

  • We do not run our own servers, and your files never pass through any server we control.
  • We do not read, view, or analyse your files or photos.
  • We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party.
  • We do not track you or serve ads.

Where your data lives

Everything you back up through HCloud stays in your own Google account, governed by Google's own privacy policy. Harmoni Cloud simply provides the interface to upload it there.

Your access token

Your Google sign-in token is stored only on your own device, used solely to authorise uploads on your behalf. You can revoke Harmoni Cloud's access at any time from your Google Account's security settings, or by disconnecting inside the app.
